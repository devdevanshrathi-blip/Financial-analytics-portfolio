01 - # Forex Strategy Risk Audit

## Objective
To evaluate the risk characteristics of a forex trading strategy and identify sources of drawdowns and volatility.

## Scope
This project focuses on analyzing a forex trading strategy to:
- Understand return consistency
- Measure risk exposure and drawdowns
- Assess strategy robustness across market conditions
